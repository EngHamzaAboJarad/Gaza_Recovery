Our Foundation

Guided by our core values and commitment to sustainable change

Our Mission

GazaRecovery empowers communities to report urgent needs and connects those reports directly to municipalities, NGOs, and field teams for verified, prioritized action. We deliver emergency aid, evidence-backed interventions, and reconstruction projects while building local capacity and accountability.

Our Vision

We envision communities rebuilt with dignity, supported by transparent systems that reduce duplication and delay, and a coordinated operational platform enabling faster, evidence-based responses. Our goal is long-term resilience through inclusive recovery and the delivery of sustainable essential services.

Our Goals

Support 25,000+ families by 2026
Complete 100+ infrastructure and community projects
Establish sustainable access to primary healthcare and clean water in targeted communities
Create 5,000+ short- and medium-term employment opportunities through reconstruction programs

Our Mission

GazaRecovery is a community-driven digital coordination platform designed to accelerate rapid humanitarian response in Gaza.
